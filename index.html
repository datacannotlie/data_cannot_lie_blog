About the Author

I am a Bachelor of Veterinary Medicine from the Federal Rural University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRRJ, 2000, RJ, Brazil) and a Bachelor of Mathematics from Estácio de Sá University (UNESA, 2024, RJ, Brazil). I earned a Master's degree in Physiology from the University of São Paulo (USP, 2002, SP, Brazil) and a Ph.D. in Physiology from the same institution (2006). In pursuit of advanced research, I completed a postdoctoral fellowship in the electrophysiology of bulbar neurons at Oregon Health and Science University (OHSU, 2008, OR, USA), followed by a postdoctoral fellowship in mathematical modeling of the cardiovascular system at the National Laboratory for Scientific Computing (LNCC, 2014, RJ, Brazil). Passionate about mathematics and physiology, my research is focused in trying to understanding the neural mechanisms that control the cardiovascular system. You can check out my CV at My CV.
